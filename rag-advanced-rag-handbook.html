What is RAG?

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) is a groundbreaking approach that combines the generative capabilities of large language models with external knowledge retrieval systems. This powerful combination enables AI applications to access up-to-date information, domain-specific knowledge, and vast document collections.

RAG systems solve the fundamental limitation of static language models by dynamically retrieving relevant information during generation, enabling more accurate, contextual, and trustworthy responses.

Key Benefits

  • Access to real-time and domain-specific information
  • Improved accuracy and reduced hallucinations
  • Scalable knowledge integration
  • Cost-effective alternative to fine-tuning
  • Transparent and explainable AI responses
  • Easy knowledge base updates
